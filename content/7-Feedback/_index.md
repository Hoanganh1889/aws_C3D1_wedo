### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment was professional, friendly, and open, allowing interns to quickly adapt and integrate into the team. Colleagues were always willing to provide support, share their experiences, and collaborate effectively, making the internship experience both productive and enjoyable.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provided clear guidance, explained technical concepts thoroughly, and was always available to answer my questions. Rather than simply providing solutions, my mentor encouraged me to think critically and solve problems independently, which greatly improved my analytical and problem-solving skills. The Team Administrator also offered comprehensive support with documentation, procedures, and administrative tasks, ensuring a smooth internship experience.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The assigned tasks were closely related to my Software Engineering studies, enabling me to apply the knowledge I had learned at university in a real-world environment. In addition, I had the opportunity to explore modern technologies, software development processes, and industry best practices, which further strengthened my professional knowledge and technical skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
The internship provided valuable opportunities to enhance both my technical expertise and soft skills. I improved my programming, analytical thinking, and problem-solving abilities, while also developing teamwork, communication, and task management skills through practical project experience.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company fostered a positive and collaborative culture built on mutual respect, teamwork, and knowledge sharing. Team members were always willing to support one another and work together toward common goals, creating a motivating and professional working environment.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company offered appropriate support for interns, including flexible working arrangements and opportunities to participate in internal training sessions, technical workshops, and company activities. These experiences allowed me to broaden my professional knowledge, strengthen my practical skills, and gain valuable industry experience.
